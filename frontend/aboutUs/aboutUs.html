FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

At VRNas, we want to make sure that you have all the information you need to make informed decisions
about our VR services. Here are some of the most common questions we receive:
What types of VR services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of VR services including VR Development, VR Design, VR Consulting, VR Games, VR Events, and VR Entertainment. Our team works with clients across education, entertainment, architecture, and corporate sectors.

How much does your VR services cost?

Our pricing starts from $99 and varies based on the scope and complexity of your project. We offer customizable packages and flexible payment options to fit any budget. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What equipment do I need to use your VR services?

The required equipment depends on the type of VR experience. We support a wide range of VR headsets including Meta Quest, HTC Vive, and PlayStation VR. Our team will advise you on the best setup for your specific needs.

Can I try out your VR services before I commit?

Yes! We offer a free initial consultation and demo session so you can experience our VR solutions firsthand before making any commitment. We also provide a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our packages.
